BLAMAGE roughly translates from German as failure from blamieren +-age, coined by German students from two French components.
Blâmer comes from Old French blasmer, which in turn derives from Late Latin blastēmāre (a variant of blasphēmāre, meaning “to blaspheme”)
Czech: blamáž | Dutch: blamage | Polish: blamaż | Slovak: blamáž
